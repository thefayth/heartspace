# Brand Style Notes

## Direction

Heartspace should feel warm, intelligent, emotionally safe, and premium without
becoming generic romance SaaS.

## Visual Qualities

- Warm rose, plum, cream, soft teal, and gentle cyan highlights.
- Human, reflective, privacy-first.
- Polished enough for public portfolio use.
- Clear enough for trust-and-safety review.

## Typography Direction

- Clean sans-serif for documentation.
- Editorial warmth in headings.
- No fake app screenshots or generated text inside images.

## Voice

- Direct.
- Consent-forward.
- Human.
- Protective of user agency.
- Clear that Heartspace does not automate or impersonate.
